About us

Welcome to Medina & Tapas, the heart of Spanish and Moroccan culinary artistry. Founded by Sara El Imrani, our restaurant is a reflection of her passion for blending two vibrant cultures into a harmonious dining experience. At Medina & Tapas, we believe that every dish tells a story. From the bustling medinas of Morocco to the lively plazas of Spain, our menu is a journey through tradition, flavor, and innovation. Using the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques, we create dishes that celebrate the bold spices of Morocco and the vibrant tastes of Spain. Our mission is simple: to offer an unforgettable dining experience where exquisite flavors meet warm, attentive hospitality. Whether you're here for a special occasion, a casual gathering, or simply to explore new tastes, we are delighted to welcome you to our table. Step into Medina & Tapas, and let us take you on a culinary journey where every bite is crafted with love and passion. ¡Bienvenidos! مرحباً!
